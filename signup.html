Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our plans

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or switch between monthly and yearly billing at any time. If you upgrade, the new features will be available immediately, and you'll be charged the prorated difference. If you downgrade, the change will take effect at the end of your current billing period.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your payment information. We never store your full credit card details on our servers – we use a trusted third-party payment processor that complies with the highest security standards (PCI DSS Level 1).

What happens when my subscription renews?

Your subscription will automatically renew at the end of your billing period (monthly or yearly). We'll send you a reminder email 7 days before renewal. You can cancel auto-renewal at any time from your account settings.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new subscribers. If you're not completely satisfied within the first 30 days, contact our support team, and we'll process a full refund of your subscription fee.
