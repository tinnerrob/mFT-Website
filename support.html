Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How secure is my financial data?

Your security is our top priority. myFinancialTracker uses bank-level 256-bit encryption to protect all your data. We never store your bank login credentials on our servers, and all data transmission is encrypted. Additionally, you can enable biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID) for an extra layer of security.

Can I use myFinancialTracker on multiple devices?

Yes! Your myFinancialTracker account allows you to sync your data across multiple iOS devices. Simply install the app on your other devices, sign in with your account, and all your financial data will be synchronized automatically.

How do I connect my bank accounts?

To connect your bank accounts, go to the "Accounts" tab in the app, tap "Add Account," and search for your financial institution. You'll then be prompted to log in using your bank credentials. myFinancialTracker uses secure APIs to connect to over 10,000 financial institutions while keeping your login details private and secure.

What's the difference between the free and premium versions?

The free version of myFinancialTracker includes basic expense tracking, budgeting, and account syncing for up to 2 accounts. The premium version adds advanced features like financial projections, unlimited account syncing, debt payoff planning, investment tracking, priority support, and no ads.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Absolutely! You can cancel your subscription at any time through your iOS device's subscription management. If you cancel, your premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period, after which your account will revert to the free tier.

How do I export my financial data?

To export your financial data, go to Settings > Data Management > Export Data. You can choose to export in CSV or PDF format, and select the date range for the export. This feature is helpful for tax preparation or if you want to analyze your data in other tools.
